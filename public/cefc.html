China EU Future City

A pilot project under the China-EU Agreement for Sustainable Urbanization

One of the major projects ESI is currently financing is the China EU Future City, an 8 km2 land development project that will be built in 6 phases in Shenzhen.

The project is being developed by ESI’s sister company, Huan De, the largest European real estate developer in China.

CEFC is a pilot project being developed under the umbrella of the China-EU Agreement on Sustainable Urbanization.

Companies that partner with ESI-TEF have the option to become partners in the CEFC as either a co-developer or office space, R&D, and other business operation services.
