Bridge Function

AGIC Capital actively supports its portfolio companies towards successful realization of growth opportunities in Europe and the Asian markets.

Small- and medium-sized companies in Europe that strive to realize their growth potential in Asia typically struggle to overcome hurdles such as cultural differences, language barriers, governmental and regulatory risks, capital requirements etc. AGIC, with its cross-border team, local presence and market expertise, provides the bridge needed to succeed in foreign markets. AGIC not only supports its portfolio companies in every step of their market entry into Asia, but also remains a close partner throughout the subsequent phase of increasing market penetration.

Our Approach

AGIC invests in successful European companies, with the mission of helping them develop into global market leaders. We are steadily looking for unique European companies, with a growing, proven and scalable business model, that include talented and experienced management teams and have a strong growth potential in Asia and beyond.

With typical investments between EUR 25–100 million, we help companies in their day-to-day business and the execution of their strategic growth plans. We fund organic business expansion and inorganic growth as well as support succession and shareholder transitions. Investments can be made in the form of minority growth capital or buyouts. AGIC can also engage in either private secondary transactions or publicly listed entities.
