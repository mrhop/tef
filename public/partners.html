Tucher Group is the holding company of the businesses of the Tucher Family, around Thomas von Tucher and his son Florian von Tucher. The Tucher are an old and traditional German family and was first mentioned in 1309. During the 14th and 15th century, the Tucher Family made its fortune by trading from the central city of Nuremberg to the rest of Europe, especially Venice, which was the European end of the Silk Road at that time. Tucher Group has 3 main pillars of businesses, that is, Project Development (Huan De Group), Investment (Euro Sino Invest Group), and Engineering (M&P Group). Tucher Group is the main shareholder and partner of Huan De Group.
